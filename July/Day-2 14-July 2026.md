## What is MBSE?

**MBSE = Model-Based Systems Engineering**

Traditional systems engineering relies heavily on documents:

```
Requirements Document
↓
Design Document
↓
Architecture Document
↓
Test Document
```


## Objective
Understand the basics of Model-Based Systems Engineering.

## Definition
MBSE is an approach where models become the primary source of system information instead of traditional documents.

## Key Concepts
- Requirements Modeling
- Structural Modeling
- Behavioral Modeling
- Verification & Validation
- Traceability

## Benefits
- Better communication
- Reduced errors
- Easier change management
- Improved traceability
